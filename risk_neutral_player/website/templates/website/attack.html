The Rebellion automatically starts in the least defended country, regardless of ownership. If all countries are defended equally, a manual roll of the dice determines where the Rebellion starts.

The Neutral Army always attacks with three dice, A single Neutral Army can also initiate or continue an attack, using two dice.

If the Neutral Army is successful in conquering the country it attacks through rebellion, popular support automatically raises one army unit to guard the country while the remaining force of attackers continues to spread revolt through the continent.

Once the rebellion has been suppressed, the Neutral forces will automatically reinforce the first country to have rebelled, with defenders equal to the number of Neutral's starting armies. For example, if Neutral started with 4 armies in each country at the beginning of the game, the new "stronghold" country will also have 4 armies, and cannot have fewer than 3. If Neutral regains control of a country it originally started with, it will also reinforce it with the same bonus.
